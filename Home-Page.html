Who We Are

The journey of INFUSING I AM began… in 2023 when two tea enthusiasts settled in North Carolina and found themselves missing a source for quality tea. Rather than settling for less, we decided to create our own! Today, we're a small, family-run company joyfully sharing our premium teas with our beloved community and beyond. Our passion for tea and the comforting ritual it brings truly warms our hearts.

Our Philosophy

With us at Infusing I AM, you'll find a delightful collection of exquisite, authentic tea blends and delicate loose-leaf teas sourced from all around the globe.
